Varadharaja temple Kanchipuram

The Varadharaja Perumal Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Vishnu located in the city of Kanchipuram, Tamil Nadu, India. It is one of the Divya Desams, the 108 temples of Vishnu believed to have been visited by the 12 poet saints, or the Alvars 1. The temple is also known as Hastagiri and Attiyuran and is one of the most sacred places for Vaishnavites
